How It Works

Rinse Rabbit is a patented device which connects to most industrial washing machines and dryers, enabling payment and activation by phone. To use a Rinse Rabbit-enabled machine, simple do the following:

Step 1: Place your laundry in the washer or dryer.

Step 2: Simply hold your phone to the Rinse Rabbit scanner to use Samsung Pay, Apple Pay, or Google Wallet.

Step 3: Congratulations! It's that easy.
