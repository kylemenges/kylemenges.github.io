Our Story

Founded in 2016, Rinse Rabbit seeks to resolve a common annoyance amongst laundromat users and business owners alike: coin payment. Rinse Rabbit's patented receiver plugs into most standard laundromat washers and dryers, and allows for quick, convenient payment via Apple Pay, Samsung Pay, or Google Wallet. Laundry owners who install Rinse Rabbit see an XX% increase in monthly sales.



Our People

Kyle Menges, CEO

Born and raised in Richmond, Virginia, Kyle worked in the entertainment industry prior to receiving his JD-MBA from Northwestern University. He really, really hated running to banks or grocery stores for coins to use in his apartment complexes, so he developed Rinse Rabbit to ease the burden.
